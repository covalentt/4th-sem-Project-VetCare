Our Story

From humble beginnings to Nepal's leading veterinary care provider

15+ Years of Excellence

Nurturing Nepal through Animal Care

At Vetcare Nepal, we are dedicated to providing exceptional care for your beloved pets. Our journey began with a passion for animals and a commitment to ensuring their health and happiness.

"As Nepal's leading animal health company, we believe in the power of compassionate care and advanced veterinary medicine to make a difference in every pet's life."

Founded with a vision to revolutionize pet healthcare in Nepal, we have grown from a small clinic to a comprehensive veterinary center offering a wide range of services designed to meet all your pet's needs.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Compassion

We treat every animal with kindness, empathy, and respect, recognizing their individual needs and the special bond they share with their owners.

Excellence

We are committed to providing the highest standard of veterinary care through ongoing education, advanced technology, and best practices.

Education

We believe in empowering pet owners with knowledge and resources to be active participants in their pets' healthcare decisions.

Integrity

We operate with honesty, transparency, and ethical standards in all aspects of our practice, building trust with our clients and community.

Community

We are dedicated to making a positive impact in our local community through outreach programs, educational initiatives, and support for animal welfare.

Innovation

We continuously seek new and improved ways to provide care, embracing advances in veterinary medicine to offer the best possible outcomes for our patients.
