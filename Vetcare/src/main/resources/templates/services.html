Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our veterinary services

We provide comprehensive veterinary care for a wide range of animals including dogs, cats, birds, small mammals (rabbits, guinea pigs, hamsters), and exotic pets.

We recommend annual wellness exams for adult pets and bi-annual exams for senior pets (typically over 7 years old).

In case of an emergency, call our clinic immediately. During operating hours, we prioritize emergency cases.

A basic grooming session typically takes between 1-3 hours, depending on the size of your pet and the services requested.

Vaccination needs vary based on your pet's species, age, lifestyle, and health status. We create customized vaccination plans during wellness visits.
